LODGINGS

Accommodation for IntegraatioFest is located at Turun Steiner-koulu(a school), Kaivokatu 12. Sleeping happens on the floor and one needs their own bedding and sleeping pad. People are divided to sleep in different spaces with other people from their own organization. Distance from the lodgings to the University is 900 meters, 1.3 km to T-talo, 1.7 km to Q-talo and 1.9 km to the city centre. Bus lines between the accommodation and city centre are numbers 32, 42, ja 53A. Bus timetables can be found at https://www.foli.fi/en or in the Föli-app.

The doors at the school are locked during the weekend and therefore one can visit the accommodation only at certain predetermined times. During these times there will be two monitors present to open the door, guide people to their sleeping spaces and answer the phone. Phone number for the accommodation is: [number].

Monitors are present at the school as follows:
Friday at 17:00-18:30 and 21:00-00:00.
Saturday at 00:00-10:00, 14:00-17:00 and 21:00-00:00.
Sunday at 00:00-11:00 (remember the end of Daylight saving time. Accommodation will close at 11:00 Eastern European Time or at 12:00 Eastern European Summer Time)

Transportation for luggage from the University to the accommodation will be organized if one does not wish to visit the accommodation before the start of Friday’s Sitz Party. On Sunday one must leave the lodgings by 12:00.

SHOWERINFO

Breakfast will be served at the accommodation on Saturday at 09:00-10:00.

Consuming alcohol or other substances or having them visible at the school premises is strictly forbidden.
